Instructions

Game Duration: 2 minutess

The Higher Score Player can win the game

Initial Player Arreibutes:

Attack Point: 100

Score: 0

Speed of movement:200

Game flow:

Pair Up

1. Pressing either "Player 1" button or "Player 2" button

2. Wait for other Player to join the game

Game Characters

Monster have 400-1000 Health Point, the last player kill the monster can earn points

Kill Monsters: + 30 scores in player scores

Collect Swords: + 200 in player attack point

Collect Gem: 10 scores

Player movements:

Player1

Move: W:"Up", A:"Left", S:"Down", D: "Right"

Attack: Q

Player2:

Move: Arrow key ↑:"Up", ←:"Left", ↓:"Down", →:"Right"

Attack: Shift

Cheat modes:

Click on Keyboard Space Key

1. The attack range is increased

2. All the monsters can be killed by one attack regardless of the levels of difficulty

3. The speed is increased

After finishing the game, there are two buttons to select

Click on "Restart" Button: back to the pair up page

Click on "Back Home" Button: back to the home page
